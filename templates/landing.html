Everything you need
when a bag goes missing

Most travellers can't accurately describe their bag under pressure. bagtag.aero fixes that.

IATA bag profiles

Store colour, material, handle type, wheel type, size category, locks, straps, and distinguishing marks — exactly what the desk will ask for.

Trip & flight tracking

Create trips, add flights, assign bags, and build a per-trip packing list. Mark items packed as you go.

Shareable QR codes

Every bag gets a unique public URL and a QR code you can print. Staff — or anyone who finds it — can see the details without an account.

Packing templates

Five ready-made packing lists (Weekend, Business, Beach, City Break, Hiking) — clone and customise for any trip.

Bag update notifications

Get in-app alerts when someone views your public bag page — useful when your bag has been found and is being processed.

Bag photos

Upload photos of your luggage so there's no ambiguity at the lost-luggage desk or when proving ownership.
